## Why This Skill Wins

**1. HEARD Framework**
Not just "sorry for the inconvenience." The HEARD framework (Disney/Ritz-Carlton standard) turns angry customers into loyal ones. Baked into every complaint prompt.

**2. Calibrated Apology Language**
Over-apologizing increases legal liability. Under-apologizing escalates the situation. This skill finds the exact language — enough empathy without admitting negligence.

**3. Legal-Safe Escalation Templates**
The legal threat and supervisor request templates are specifically written to avoid creating paper trails that hurt you in disputes. No competitor at $29 offers this.

**4. Proactive > Reactive**
Most support tools are reactive. Prompt 4 generates outbound templates that get ahead of delays, defects, and unhappy NPS scores before customers escalate. This reduces inbound volume — a business outcome, not just a writing tool.

**5. Works in Any Stack**
Gmail, Zendesk, Gorgias, Intercom, Freshdesk, Outlook, Help Scout — paste the template anywhere. No integration, no API, no platform dependency.
