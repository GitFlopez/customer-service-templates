## The 4 Prompts

### Prompt 1 (Free) — Complaint Handler
Generates empathy-first complaint responses using the HEARD framework. Includes:
- Opening acknowledgment (validates without admitting fault)
- Empathy statement (mirrors customer emotion)
- Ownership + apology (calibrated to severity)
- Resolution offer (3 options: fix, replace, refund)
- Recovery gesture (goodwill offer to retain the customer)
- Relationship close (invites continued dialogue)

### Prompt 2 — Return / Refund / Exchange Script
Generates policy-forward but empathy-driven return/refund responses. Includes:
- Policy-compliant response that doesn't sound robotic
- Approval path (when to approve, how to word it)
- Denial path (how to say no without losing the customer)
- Exchange upsell (turn a return into a swap + upgrade)
- Re-engagement close (discount for next order)
- 3 subject line variants for email chains

### Prompt 3 — Escalation & Difficult Customer Playbook
Generates templates for high-tension situations. Includes:
- De-escalation email (angry customer, threats to leave)
- Supervisor request response (when customer demands a manager)
- Social media crisis DM → private channel redirect
- Legal threat / formal complaint received response
- Repeat complainant response (boundary-setting with empathy)
- Internal escalation handoff note (for the next agent)

### Prompt 4 — Proactive Service Scripts
Generates outbound customer service templates (prevent problems before they happen). Includes:
- Order delay notification (before the customer notices)
- Shipping issue proactive outreach (carrier problem detected)
- Product defect batch notification (recall or quality issue)
- Win-back after a bad experience (48-hour follow-up)
- Loyalty milestone outreach (100th order, 1-year anniversary)
- NPS recovery (after a low score, before churn happens)
